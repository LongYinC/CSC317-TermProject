Frequently Ask Questions

Shipping

Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to most countries. Delivery takes 3-7 business days.
What are shipping costs?
Free shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping is $5 for orders under $50.
Why is my order coming in separate packages, on seperate days?
We have 8 different warehouses across the U.S. in order to get your items to you as fast as possible! However, sometimes products from the same order are shipped from different warehouses. This does not affect your shipping cost, but may require the order to be delivered in multiple packages and the shipping time or carrier by shipment invoice may vary.

Returns

What is your return policy?
30-day returns for unworn items with original tags. Full refund or exchange available.
How do I return an item?
Email support@cscstudio.com to get a return authorization and shipping label.
